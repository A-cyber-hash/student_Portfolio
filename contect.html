About Me

Hello! I'm Amir Khan, a passionate and curious tech enthusiast from Auriaya, India. I'm currently pursuing a Bachelor's in Computer Science from Lamrin Tech Skills University, and I’m driven by my love for learning, building, and breaking things — especially in the fields of web development and cybersecurity.

Ever since I first wrote a few lines of HTML, I knew tech was my thing. I’ve been diving deeper ever since — whether it's developing clean, responsive websites, exploring ethical hacking through Kali Linux, or scripting automation tools using Python and Bash.

My journey isn't just about code — it's about solving problems, pushing limits, and constantly improving. Whether I'm freelancing on web projects or sharpening my skills in penetration testing labs, I bring energy, curiosity, and a commitment to quality in everything I do.
